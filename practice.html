how to build sustainable internal motivation

There is an idea called SELF-DETERIMINATION theory that describes three essential needs:
  1. autonomy-you need to feel like you are in control of your learning and your goal; when we feel like our actions lead to real change, we are more likely to immerse ourselves in our work.

  2. competency-you need to feel that you are making progress and you are able to learn the skills and get better; when we feel like we have what takes to succed, we are more likely to show up consistently.

  3. connection-we need to experience a sense of belonging to other people; if we have the same goals, we can use one another for motivation.



P.S: We learn because we love to learn, not to escape any punishments or to get any reward.
